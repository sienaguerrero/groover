How Groover Works

Groover is a web application that implements a deep learning neural network to generate music recommendations based on your favorite song's lyrical content. We measure document similarity between your input song’s lyrics and the lyrics of 55,000 songs on our pre-trained neural network model, then give you the top matches.

This web app was developed by Siena Guerrero, Trevor Walker, Walker Quinn, Teresa Ibarra, and Jocelyn Chen for their Spring 2019 CS121 (“Software Development”) project at Harvey Mudd College.
